How EMI Works

EMI is calculated using reducing balance method. Each monthly payment includes interest + principal repayment.

FAQ

What is EMI?
EMI is Equated Monthly Installment paid every month.

Is EMI fixed?
Yes for fixed-rate loans, EMI remains constant.

Does EMI reduce principal?
Yes, part of EMI reduces outstanding loan principal.
