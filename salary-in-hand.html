How it works

Your annual CTC is divided into monthly salary. Tax is estimated and deducted to calculate take-home pay.

FAQ

What is in-hand salary?
The amount you receive in your bank account after deductions.

Is CTC equal to salary?
No, CTC includes benefits and taxes. In-hand is lower.

Is this accurate?
It is an estimate for financial planning.
