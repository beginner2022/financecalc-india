How Salary is Calculated

Your annual CTC is divided into monthly gross salary. Then tax and PF deductions are applied to calculate your in-hand salary.

This tool gives an estimated take-home salary for financial planning.

What is In-Hand Salary?

In-hand salary is the actual amount credited to your bank account after deductions like tax, PF, and other contributions.

It is usually lower than CTC (Cost to Company), which includes benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CTC equal to in-hand salary?
No, CTC includes bonuses and employer contributions.

How accurate is this calculator?
It gives a close estimate based on inputs, but actual salary may vary.

What deductions are included?
Tax and PF deductions are considered.
